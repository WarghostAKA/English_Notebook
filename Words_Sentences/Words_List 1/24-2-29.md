## Word List 1

## 2. accord 
### vt. 一致
他们的实验结果与我们预测的相当一致
**These results accord closely with our predictions.**

### n. 协议
我们达成了一份协议，收益应当均分
**We came to an accord that profits should be share equally.**

### with one accord 一致地
他们步调一致地走过草坪
**With one accord they walkd back over the grass.**

### in accord with 与……一致
大多数人在渴望和平这一点上是一致的
**Most people are in accord with their desire for peace.**
